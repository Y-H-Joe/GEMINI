Annotation: The OUTPOST_biomarker_scores.{group1}_vs_{group2}.{taxa_level}.tsv files contain the biomarkers for all group pairs across all taxa levels. The biomarkers are ranked from higer OUTPOST_biomarker_score to lower. The basic idea of OUTPOST_biomarker_score is meta-analysis. The score will add one if the biomarker passed one criteria. The criteria include taxa_abun_top_100, taxa_abun_signicant, LDA_larger_than_2, virulence_top_100, plasmid_top_100, antibiotic_top_100, ANCOM_identified.
